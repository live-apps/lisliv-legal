Lisliv Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Lisliv is a personal organizer application designed with a privacy-first philosophy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All your data — including tasks, medications, shopping lists, notes, and voice recordings — is stored exclusively on your device. Lisliv does not use servers, accounts, or cloud synchronization.

Voice & AI Processing

Lisliv uses AI services to process voice input. Your voice is transcribed and processed using the provider you select (Groq or Google Gemini) with your own API key.

Groq: https://groq.com/privacy-policy

Google Gemini: https://ai.google.dev/terms

Lisliv does not access, store, or transmit your voice recordings or transcripts.

API Keys

Your API keys are securely stored on your device using Android's encrypted storage. Lisliv does not access or transmit your API keys.

Medications & Health Data

Any medication or health-related information you enter remains strictly on your device. Lisliv does not access, collect, or transmit health data.

No Ads or Tracking

Lisliv contains no advertisements and does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party monitoring of any kind.

Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: us.codehelp@gmail.com
