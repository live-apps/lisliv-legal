Lisliv Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

Lisliv is a personal organizer app built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

Data Storage

All your data — tasks, medications, shopping lists, notes, and voice recordings — is stored exclusively on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no cloud sync.

Voice & AI Processing

Lisliv uses AI to organize your voice input. Your voice is transcribed and processed by the AI provider you choose (Groq or Google Gemini) using your own API key. Your data is governed by the privacy policy of your chosen provider:

Groq: console.groq.com/privacy

Google Gemini: ai.google.dev/terms

We never see, access, or store your voice recordings or transcripts.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored securely on your device using Android's encrypted storage. We never transmit or access your keys.

Medications & Health Data

Medication and health information you enter stays on your device only. We never access or transmit health data.

No Ads, No Tracking

Lisliv contains no advertisements and no analytics or tracking of any kind.
